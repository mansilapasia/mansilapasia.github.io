The Land of Gods

Himachal Pradesh is a northern Indian state in the Himalayas. It's home to scenic mountain towns and resorts such as Dalhousie. Host to the Dalai Lama, Himachal Pradesh has a strong Tibetan presence. This is reflected in its Buddhist temples and monasteries, as well as its vibrant Tibetan New Year celebrations. The region is also well known for its trekking, climbing and skiing areas.

Himachal Pradesh Tour

INR 42,000

  • Chandigarh
  • Manali
  • Shimla

     This Tours starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 8.

       Day 1   Chandigarh

Arrive Ambala Cantt Railway Station / Chandigarh airport And reach our hotel at your own cost (hotel check-in time 12.00 noon) After Lunch Visit Rock Garden, Rose Garden. Night Halt at Chandigarh.

       Day 2   Chandigarh - Manali

After breakfast proceed to Manali (10 hrs). Enroute Lunch will be Provided. Night Halt at Manali.

       Day 3   Manali

After breakfast visit Solang Valley (known as shooting point) and enjoy Ski Himalayan Cable Car Ride (at our cost). After lunch day is free for Shopping at Famous mall road. Night halt at Manali.

       Day 4   Manali

After breakfast full day visit at snow point (till our vehice can reach) i.e. Rohtang / Madhi / Gulaba (Note: As per NGT rules Snow Point remains closed on Tuesday). Night halt at Manali.

       Day 5   Manali

After Breakfast visit 5000 thousand years old Gaytri Devi Temple (Build By Pandvas) and famous Hadimba Devi Temple . After Lunch Day is Free for Rest. Night halt at Manali.

       Day 6   Manali - Shimla

After early breakfast proceed to Shimla (10 hrs. journey), enroute we will visit Hanogi Mata temple thereafter continue our journey towards Shimla. Lunch will be Provided Enroute. Night halt at Hotel.

       Day 7   Shimla

After breakfast visit Kufri, Mini Zoo and enjoy horse riding (at your own cost) Enjoy Natural Beauty. After lunch we will have free time for shopping at Mall Road. Night halt at Shimla.

       Day 8   Shimla – Chandigarh / Ambala Drop

After breakfast proceed to Ambala (4 hrs) en route visit pinjore Garden. Lunch will be en route & thereafter drop at Ambala Railway station to board train 12472 Swaraj Express at 17.55 hrs with memorable experience of Meraki.Passengers booking flight / train from Ambala / Chandigarh will have to take taxi from shimla or chandigarh will have to take taxi from Shimla or Chandigarh at your own cost. (Flight to book after 3.00 pm)
