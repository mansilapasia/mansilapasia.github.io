The Land of Gods

Himachal Pradesh is a northern Indian state in the Himalayas. It's home to scenic mountain towns and resorts such as Dalhousie. Host to the Dalai Lama, Himachal Pradesh has a strong Tibetan presence. This is reflected in its Buddhist temples and monasteries, as well as its vibrant Tibetan New Year celebrations. The region is also well known for its trekking, climbing and skiing areas.

     This Tours starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 11.

       Day 1   Arrive Amritsar

Arrive Amritsar station/airport and reach the hotel (at your own, Check in time at 12.00 noon). After Lunch at 02:30 pm visit (India–Pakistan) Wagha Border, to see the Flag retreat Ceremony performed by the Jawans of the Border Security Force. Return to the Hotel. Night Halt at Amritsar.

       Day 2   Amritsar – Dalhousie

After breakfast visit Golden Temple and Jalianwala Bagh. After lunch proceed to Dalhousie (7–8 hrs. journey), Night halt at Dalhousie.

       Day 3   Dalhousie – Khajjiar – Dalhousie

After breakfast we visit Khajjiar, (If road is open). which is also known as Mini Swiss for a breath taking view of the ground covered with pine trees. After lunch day is free to enjoy magnificent climate of this exotic destinations. Night halt at Dalhousie.

       Day 4   Dalhousie – Dharamshala

After early breakfast we proceed to Dharamshala by road (7–8 hrs. journey). After Lunch visit to Bhagsunath Temple, and Dalai Lama Temple, Dinner and night halt. At Dharamshala.

       Day 5   Dharamshala – Manali

Afterbreakfast Proceed to Manali.(10 to12 hrs Journey.)Lunch will be Provided Enroute.Night halt at Manali.

       Day 6   Manali

After breakfast visit Solang Valley (known as shooting point) and enjoy Ski Himalayan Cable Car Ride (at our cost) and also visit the famous Hadimba Devi Temple. After lunch day is free for Rest/Shopping. Night halt at Manali.

       Day 7   Manali

After breakfast full day visit at snow point ( till our vehicle can reach) Rohtang / Madhi / Gulaba (Note: Snow point remains Closed on Tuesday). Night halt at Manali.

       Day 8   Manali – Shimla

After early breakfast proceed to Shimla (10 hrs. journey), enroute we will visit Hanogi Mata temple thereafter continue our journey towards Shimla. Lunch will be Provided Enroute. Night halt at Hotel.

       Day 9   Shimla

After breakfast visit Kufri, Mini Zoo and enjoy horse riding (at your own cost) Enjoy Natural Beauty. After lunch we will have free time for shopping at Mall Road. Night halt at Shimla.

       Day 10   Shimla – Chandigarh

After breakfast proceed to Chandigarh (4 hrs). After lunch sightseeing of Pinjore Garden and Rock Garden. Night halt at Chandigarh.

       Day 11   Chandigarh

After breakfast drop at Chandigarh airport till 10.00 am with memorable experience of the tour conducted by Meraki.
