Enchanting Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu- A land of cultural and religious heritage, offers every chance to its tourists and pilgrims to be enchanted with the vast plethora of history and beauty that it has. It is a blend of both- the modern and the ancient, which makes Tamil Nadu a beautiful destination to explore and experience our past cultural heritage with all the amenities of the present.

South Tour

INR 44,000

  • Ooty
  • Kodaikanal

     This Tours starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 6.

       Day 1   Coimbatore - Ooty

Arrive Coimbatore airport & proceed to Ooty (3hrs. journey), In the evening enjoy boating at Ooty Lake (at your own cost). Night halt at Ooty.

       Day 2   Ooty

After breakfast visit to Doddapetta Peak, Highest peak in south India (8642 ft.), Tea Museum, Tea Factory, and Centenary Rose Garden to explore the largest collections of Roses in India. After lunch visit to Botanical Garden.

       Day 3   Ooty - Kodaikanal

Early morning proceed to Kodaikanal (8 -10 hrs. journey), breakfast and lunch will be provided en route journey. Night halt at Kodaikanal.

       Day 4   Kodaikanal

After breakfast sightseeing of Coaker’s Walk and Brayant’s Park. After lunch visit Kodai Lake enjoy boating (at your own cost). Night halt at Kodaikanal.

       Day 5   Kodaikanal

After breakfast visit Pillar Rock, Green valley view, Silver Cascade waterfalls and Upper Lake View. After lunch free for leisure activities. Night halt at Kodaikanal.

       Day 6   Kodaikanal – Coimbatore Drop

After breakfast proceed to Coimbatore to board your flight. Tour ends with sweet memories conducted by Meraki.
