Sikkim - Small But Beautiful

Sikkim is a state in northeast India, bordered by Bhutan, Tibet and Nepal. Part of the Himalayas, the area has a dramatic landscape that includes India’s highest mountain, 8,586m Kangchenjunga. Sikkim is also home to glaciers, alpine meadows and thousands of varieties of wildflowers. Steep paths lead to hilltop Buddhist monasteries such as Pemayangtse, which dates to the early 1700s.

Exotic Sikkim Tour

INR 67,000

  • Darjeeling
  • Pelling
  • Namchi (Chardham)
  • Gangtok
  • Lachung
  • Yumthang

     This Tours starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 11.

       Day 1   Bagdogra - Darjeeling

Arrive Bagdogra airport /New Jailpaiguri station have lunch & proceed to Darjeeling (4 hrs. journey), Arrive Darjeeling and free time for rest; evening is free for shopping at Mall Road. Night halt at Darjeeling.

       Day 2   Darjeeling

Early morning at 03.30 hrs. proceed to Tiger Hill to see the breath taking view of sunrise & also the most magnificent view of Kanchanjunga Range. After breakfast full day sightseeing of Darjeeling which includes Rock Garden, Darjeeling Zoo, Tenzing Rock & Refuge Centre. Night halt at Darjeeling.

       Day 3   Darjeeling - Pelling

After breakfast proceed to Pelling (8 hrs. journey), en route enjoy River Rafting (at your own cost). Arrive Pelling and rest of the day is free at leisure. Night halt at Pelling.

       Day 4   Pelling

After breakfast sightseeing of Kecheopalri Lake & Kanchanjunga water Falls. After lunch visit Pemyang Monastery. Night halt at Pelling.

       Day 5   Pelling - Gangtok

After breakfast we will depart for Gangtok (7-8 hrs. journeys) Enroute visit the famous Namchi Chardham temple Arrive Gangtok and rest of the day is free at leisure. Night halt at Gangtok.

       Day 6   Gangtok

After breakfast we proceed to visit Tshangu lake situated at 12,000 feet above sea level in the eastern part of Sikkim which is known for its natural beauty. Night halt at Gangtok.

       Day 7   Gangtok – Lachung

After early lunch we will depart for Lachung (6 hrs. journey) en route enjoy natural beauty of Seven Sister Water Falls & Mangan View. Night halt at Lachung.

       Day 8   Lachung – Yumthang - Lachung

Morning to afternoon explore the Natural beauty of Yumthang Valley & Yumi Somdom (Zero Point). Night halt at Lachung.

       Day 9   Lachung – Gangtok

After breakfast we will proceed to Gangtok (6 hrs. journey). Arrive Gangtok and rest of the day is free at leisure. Night halt at Gangtok.

       Day 10   Gangtok

After breakfast sightseeing of Gangtok which includes International Flower Show, Vanjageri Falls, museum , Stupa and also enjoy Gondola Cable Car Ride. After lunch free time for shopping at the famous M.G. Road. Night halt at Gangtok.

       Day 11   Gangtok – Bagdogra / New Jailpaiguri Drop

After breakfast at 06:00 am transfer to New Jalpaigudi station /Bagdogra airport (Flight/Train To Be Booked after 14:00 onwards) with memorable experience of the tour conducted by Meraki.
